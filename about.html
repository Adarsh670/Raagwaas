About

At Raagwaas, we weave together the richness of India's cultural heritage to create a world of bespoke design and timeless style. Our artistry and devotion to craftsmanship bring to life fashion and lifestyle aspirations that embody the essence of Indian heritage. We are a testament to the beauty and diversity of our timeless traditions.

Raagwaas

Our vision is to immerse the world in the stunning hues and intricate designs of Indian textile culture. Our mission is to ignite a lasting impact by fostering growth and prosperity for our clients and artisans. We strive to create bespoke designs that not only fulfil people's fashion and lifestyle aspirations but also play a role in nation-building, craft sustainability, and social welfare.

In a world of shifting fashion trends, the essence of tradition endures.

our

heritage

Nestled amid the illustrious history of the fashion industry, Surya stands four generations tall and strong, faithful in its goal for a world with a spotlight on the Indian heritage. Raghav Mittal developed Raagwaas as a symbol of exclusivity and expertise in bridal wear. It is a priceless jewel in the legacy of the House of Surya that we cherish and are dedicated to maintaining for future generations.

"Raagwaas was born out of my love for Indian classical music, with a hope to reflect it in the bespoke designs that adorn an Indian bride on the day of her wedding. My inspiration to bring the bearers of delicate crafts under one roof is my father, who has more than 30 years of experience working with craftsmen and artisans. Raagwaas is a place for nurturing these craftsmen and their craft, woven in the fabric of timeless fashion statements to celebrate the bride."

Raghav Mittal , COO.
