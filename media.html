EVENT

Raagwaas Launch, Dec 17,

The grand opening of Raagwaas, a purveyor of high fashion that takes Indian craftsmanship to new heights, took place on December 17th, 2022. Guests were immersed in a world of magnificent beauty and unrivalled refinement at their debuting showcase at the Oberoi Ballroom, the perfect setting to display the lavishness of Raagwaas' exquisite wedding clothing. Starting in Delhi's Chandni Chowk, Raagwaas has moved to expand the legacy of the house of Surya all around the world.

Raagwaas is committed to preserving and showcasing Indian culture via its couture, serving as a cultural custodian for Indian artistry. Follow us on Instagram to see how we bring the beauty of Indian art, culture, and wedding attire to life via visual storytelling. Let us be your guide through this incredible experience of once in a lifetime moment by contacting us at press@raagwaas.com if you have any questions or would want to learn more.
